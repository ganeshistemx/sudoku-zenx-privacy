How to delete your Sudoku ZenX data

App: Sudoku ZenX · Developer: Ganesh Sharma · Last updated: 27 May 2026

This page explains exactly how to delete the data that the Sudoku ZenX app (package com.zenx.sudoku), published by Ganesh Sharma, has collected about you. There are two ways: in-app (instant, recommended) and by email (we process within 30 days).

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open Sudoku ZenX on your device.
  2. Tap the menu icon (top-left of the home screen).
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Scroll to the Data section.
  5. Tap Reset all progress (red button) and confirm twice.

This immediately:

  • Clears all on-device data (game progress, statistics, settings, achievements, themes).
  • Deletes your cloud record from our Firebase Firestore database, including users/<your-id>, your public leaderboard entry leaderboard/<your-id>, and your reserved nickname.
  • Cancels any scheduled daily-reminder notifications.

Option 2 — Email request

If you've already uninstalled the app or cannot access it, email us:

To: ganesh.istemx@gmail.com
Subject: Sudoku ZenX — Data deletion request
Include: Your leaderboard nickname, or the Google account email you used to sign in to Google Play Games inside the app.

We will confirm deletion within 30 days (typically within a few business days).

Just want to stop using the app?

Uninstalling Sudoku ZenX deletes all on-device data but does not delete your cloud record. To fully erase your data, use Option 1 (Reset all progress) before uninstalling, or use Option 2 after.
